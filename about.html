Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Priscilla! I am an artist born and raised in the Southern California sunshine. I enjoy creating many different types of art but my main art medium is oil paint. I specialize in painting on wood, allowing the natural beauty of the wood grain to enhance my work. My work is often inspired by colors, texture, and ambiance.
